# Play Store Descriptions

## Short Description 
Defend, build, and upgrade in a compact old castle keep to hold of the forces of the dead for mobile.

## Long Description
Reclaim your castle and defend against waves of enemies in this mobile prototype. Earn points (Valor) by defeating enemies, then spend them to recruit old castle staff to your keep. Simple gameplay designed to keep you on the edge of your seat as you try to hold your last stand against evil.

**Features:**
- Hold down to rebuild walls/doors and recruit units  
- Fast-paced, wave-based gameplay    
- Works on Android (ARM64 devices)
